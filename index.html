Safety Tips and Anti-Scam Reminders

  • Meet in public campus areas and avoid isolated places during handovers.
  • Do not share full IDs, passwords, or one-time codes to claim items.
  • Ask for proof of ownership details before returning valuables.
  • Never send money to process a claim. FoundU does not charge return fees.
  • Report suspicious behavior to admin immediately.
